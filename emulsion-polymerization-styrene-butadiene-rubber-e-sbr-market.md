The E-SBR market can be categorized into two main types: oil-extended and non-oil extended. Oil-extended E-SBR incorporates a process oil, enhancing flexibility and lowering production costs, making it suitable for tire and automotive applications. Non-oil extended E-SBR, lacking additional oils, offers improved wear resistance and is preferred for high-performance applications in industries like adhesives and coatings.</p></p>
