<p><ul><li>Hammer Drills</li><li>Impact Drills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corded drill market is primarily divided into two key types: hammer drills and impact drills. Hammer drills are designed for drilling into hard materials, using a combination of rotary motion and a hammering action to break through tough surfaces like concrete. In contrast, impact drills focus on providing high torque for driving screws and fasteners, utilizing a concussive force to maximize efficiency in fastening tasks. Both types offer distinct advantages for specific applications in construction and DIY projects.</p></p>
