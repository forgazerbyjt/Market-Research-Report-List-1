<p><strong>The Molecular Beacon Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tag Molecular Beacon</li><li>Label-free Molecular Beacon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular beacon probe market features two primary types: Tag Molecular Beacons and Label-free Molecular Beacons. Tag Molecular Beacons, which are labeled with fluorescent dyes, provide a visual signal upon hybridization with target nucleic acids, enabling real-time detection and quantification. In contrast, Label-free Molecular Beacons rely on changes in molecular properties for detection, eliminating the need for external labels. Both types are used in various applications, including diagnostics, research, and environmental monitoring, enhancing sensitivity and specificity in molecular analysis.</p></p>
