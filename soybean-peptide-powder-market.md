<p><p>The soybean peptide powder market is categorized into three main types: endogenous peptide powder, synthetic peptide powder, and enzymatic peptide powder. Endogenous peptide powder is derived naturally from soybeans during processing, retaining inherent nutritional benefits. Synthetic peptide powder is artificially created in laboratories and tailored for specific functionalities. Enzymatic peptide powder is produced through enzymatic hydrolysis, enhancing digestibility and bioavailability. Each type offers unique applications in food, health supplements, and cosmetics, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
