<p><ul><li>Single Screen</li><li>Double Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart cash registers can be categorized into two main types: single screen and double screen. Single screen registers feature a single interface for both cashiers and customers, streamlining transactions but limiting customer interaction. In contrast, double screen registers incorporate one display for the cashier and another for the customer, enhancing engagement and allowing customers to view transaction details in real-time. This dual-display setup can improve transparency, boost customer satisfaction, and facilitate contactless payments, catering to modern retail demands.</p></p>
