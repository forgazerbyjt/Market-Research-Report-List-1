Hydraulic machines leverage high-pressure systems to compress biomass more tightly, ideal for dense briquettes. Screw extrusion machines use a rotating screw mechanism to push materials through a die, producing uniform shapes and sizes. Each type serves specific market needs, optimizing biomass utilization.</p></p>
