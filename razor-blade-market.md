<p><p>The razor blade market is primarily divided into two types: double edge and single edge blades. Double edge razor blades are designed for use with safety razors, offering a cost-effective solution with a sharp edge on both sides, allowing for a closer shave and longer usability. In contrast, single edge razor blades are often used in straight razors or some cartridge systems, providing a straightforward design with a single sharp edge, ideal for precision grooming. Both types cater to distinct user preferences and shaving techniques.</p></p>
