<p><strong>The Silicone Rubber Tracheostomy Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Cannulas</li><li>Inner Cannulas</li><li>Obturators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone rubber tracheostomy tube market consists of three primary types: outer cannulas, inner cannulas, and obturators. Outer cannulas serve as the primary structure, providing stability and maintaining airway patency. Inner cannulas fit inside the outer cannula and can be easily replaced or cleaned, allowing for improved hygiene and durability. Obturators facilitate the insertion of the outer cannula into the trachea, ensuring ease of placement. Together, these components enhance patient care and support efficient respiratory management.</p></p>
