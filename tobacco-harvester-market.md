<p><p>The tobacco harvester market is primarily divided into two types: self-propelled tobacco harvesters and walk-behind tobacco harvesters. Self-propelled tobacco harvesters are larger, utilizing advanced technology for efficient harvesting, often featuring automated systems for speed and productivity, suitable for larger tobacco farms. In contrast, walk-behind tobacco harvesters are smaller, manually operated machines designed for smaller plots. They offer versatility and ease of use, making them ideal for smaller-scale operations or diverse terrains where larger harvesters may struggle.</p></p>
