<p><p>The water filtration systems market comprises various technologies designed to purify water. Reverse osmosis (RO) water filters utilize a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants, ensuring high-quality drinking water by eliminating impurities and dissolved solids. Ultrafiltration membranes operate at a higher pore size, effectively removing bacteria and larger particulates while retaining beneficial minerals. Other filtration methods include carbon filters, UV purification, and ceramic filters, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences for clean, safe drinking water.</p></p>
