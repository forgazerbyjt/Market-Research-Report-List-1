<p><p>Manual machine tool chucks are essential fixtures for holding workpieces during machining operations. The 2-jaw chuck is typically used for irregularly shaped parts, providing quick setup and clamping. The 3-jaw chuck is the most common type, offering self-centering capabilities for circular workpieces, ensuring uniform grip. The 4-jaw chuck allows for greater precision and flexibility, as each jaw can be adjusted independently, making it ideal for complex or non-standard shapes. Each type caters to specific machining needs, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
