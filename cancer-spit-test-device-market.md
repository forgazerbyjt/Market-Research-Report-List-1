<p><p>The Cancer Spit Test Device Market encompasses two primary collection methods: passive drool collection and oral swab collection. Passive drool collection involves individuals naturally allowing saliva to accumulate in a container, enabling non-invasive sampling without active participation. In contrast, oral swab collection uses a device resembling a cotton swab to actively collect saliva from the inner cheeks or gums, ensuring a more targeted sample. Both methods aim to facilitate early cancer detection with user-friendly and accessible testing options.</p></p>
