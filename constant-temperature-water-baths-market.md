<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Baths</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The constant temperature water baths market includes two primary types: circulating and non-circulating water baths. Circulating water baths maintain uniform temperature throughout the bath by continuously moving the water, ensuring consistent thermal conditions, which is crucial for sensitive experiments. In contrast, non-circulating water baths rely on natural convection for temperature stability, making them simpler but potentially less uniform. Both types serve various applications in laboratories, industrial processes, and educational settings, catering to specific needs based on temperature precision and fluid movement.</p></p>
