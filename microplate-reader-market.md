<p><strong>The Microplate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate reader market features two primary types: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter microplate readers utilize specific optical filters to isolate wavelengths for absorbance or fluorescence detection, making them suitable for straightforward assays. On the other hand, optical grating microplate readers employ diffraction gratings to separate light into its component wavelengths, allowing for more versatile and sensitive measurements across a broader spectrum. Both types are essential for various applications in laboratories and research settings.</p></p>
