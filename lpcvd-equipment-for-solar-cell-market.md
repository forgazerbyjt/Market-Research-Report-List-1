<p><p>LPCVD (Low-Pressure Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment plays a crucial role in the solar cell market by enabling the deposition of thin films essential for photovoltaic performance. Tube LPCVD systems utilize long, horizontal tubes for processing, offering efficient thermal control and scalability. In contrast, non-tube LPCVD systems employ batch processing in enclosed chambers, providing versatility and rapid throughput. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-efficiency solar cells, enhancing performance while minimizing manufacturing costs.</p></p>
