<p><strong>The Artificial Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Single Chamber Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Double-Chamber Cardiac Pacing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial pacemaker market includes various types, each serving specific cardiac pacing needs. Temporary cardiac pacing is used in emergency situations for short-term support. Single chamber cardiac pacing stimulates either the right atrium or ventricle, ideal for simple arrhythmias. Double-chamber cardiac pacing involves leads in both atrium and ventricle, promoting synchronized heart activity, suitable for more complex issues. Other types encompass advanced technologies like biventricular pacing and wireless pacemakers, catering to diverse patient requirements and enhancing treatment outcomes.</p></p>
