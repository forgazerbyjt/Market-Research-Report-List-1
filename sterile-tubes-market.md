<p><ul><li>Rounding Bottom Sterile Tubes</li><li>Conic Bottom Sterile Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterile tubes market includes various types, notably rounding bottom sterile tubes and conic bottom sterile tubes. Rounding bottom sterile tubes are designed to minimize the risk of contamination and facilitate mixing, making them ideal for diverse laboratory applications. In contrast, conic bottom sterile tubes feature a tapered design that allows for efficient sedimentation and easier collection of samples, particularly in centrifugation processes. Both types are essential for maintaining sample integrity and accuracy in various scientific and medical settings.</p></p>
