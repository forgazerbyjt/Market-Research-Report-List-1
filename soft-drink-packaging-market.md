<p><p>The soft drink packaging market comprises two main types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. Flexible packaging, such as pouches and bags, offers lightweight, space-saving options that can reduce shipping costs. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and environmental considerations, with rigid options often seen as premium, while flexible packaging focuses on convenience and sustainability.</p></p>
