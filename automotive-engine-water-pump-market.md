<p><ul><li>Mechanical Water Pump</li><li>Electric Water Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine water pump market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical water pumps and electric water pumps. Mechanical water pumps are driven directly by the engine’s crankshaft, relying on a belt or pulley, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Conversely, electric water pumps are powered by an electric motor, allowing for variable flow rates and improved efficiency, contributing to better engine cooling and reduced emissions. Both types serve the crucial function of circulating coolant to maintain optimal engine temperatures.</p></p>
