<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoder</li><li>Incremental Linear Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical incremental encoder market is divided into two main types: incremental rotary encoders and incremental linear encoders. Incremental rotary encoders detect rotational position changes, providing precise feedback for motors and drives. They are commonly used in robotics and automation systems. In contrast, incremental linear encoders measure linear displacement, translating motion in straight lines, often utilized in CNC machines and industrial automation. Both types leverage optical technology to ensure high accuracy and resolution in position sensing applications.</p></p>
