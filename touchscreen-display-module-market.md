<p><p>The touchscreen display module market is primarily segmented into capacitive and resistive types. Capacitive touchscreen display modules use a conductive layer to detect touch through the electrical properties of the human finger, offering high sensitivity and clarity, making them popular in smartphones and tablets. In contrast, resistive touchscreen display modules consist of multiple layers that register touch through pressure, providing durability and functionality in various environments, often used in industrial and medical applications. Each type serves distinct user needs and preferences.</p></p>
