Conical bottom silos have a tapered base, facilitating easier discharge and reducing the risk of material stagnation, making them suitable for bulk materials that require frequent handling. Other types may include hybrid designs or specialized silos tailored for specific industries or materials, reflecting diverse storage needs.</p></p>
