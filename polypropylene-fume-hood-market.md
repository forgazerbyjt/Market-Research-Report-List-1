<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hood</li><li>Ducted Fume Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polypropylene Fume Hood market comprises two main types: ductless and ducted fume hoods. Ductless fume hoods are self-contained units that filter and recirculate air, suitable for labs with limited space or where permanent ducting is impractical. In contrast, ducted fume hoods are connected to external ventilation systems, expelling contaminated air outside and providing enhanced safety for users handling hazardous chemicals. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring safety and compliance in various laboratory environments.</p></p>
