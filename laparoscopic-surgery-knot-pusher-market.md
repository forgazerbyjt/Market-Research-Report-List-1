<p><p>Laparoscopic surgery knot pushers are specialized tools used to facilitate suturing in minimally invasive procedures. The market can be divided into two main types: with suture opening and without suture opening. Knot pushers with suture opening allow surgeons to secure knots while maintaining tension on the suture, enhancing precision. In contrast, those without suture opening are designed for more straightforward applications, providing an effective means to push and secure knots without additional features. Both types cater to various surgical requirements and preferences.</p></p>
