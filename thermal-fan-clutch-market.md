<p><p>The thermal fan clutch market is primarily segmented into two types: reverse rotation and standard rotation clutches. Standard rotation clutches, on the other hand, are used in most conventional engines that rotate clockwise. Each type serves to control the cooling fan's operation based on temperature, ensuring optimal engine performance and efficiency while managing airflow effectively.</p></p>
