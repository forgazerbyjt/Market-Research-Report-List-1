<p><ul><li>Smartcard CAS</li><li>Cardless CAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conditional Access System (CAS) market is divided into two main types: Smartcard CAS and Cardless CAS. Smartcard CAS utilizes physical smartcards to authenticate and decrypt digital content, offering a secure way to control access to pay-TV and streaming services. In contrast, Cardless CAS relies on software-based solutions and internet protocols, allowing for access without the need for physical cards. This approach is often more flexible and scalable, catering to modern streaming demands and diverse devices, enhancing user experience.</p></p>
