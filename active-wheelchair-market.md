<p><p>The active wheelchair market encompasses two main types: manual wheelchairs and power wheelchairs. Manual wheelchairs are designed for individuals who prefer physical activity, allowing users to propel themselves using hand rims. They are often lighter and more portable. In contrast, power wheelchairs are motorized, providing enhanced mobility and convenience for users with limited upper body strength or those requiring assistance for longer distances. Both types cater to diverse user needs, balancing independence, comfort, and mobility.</p></p>
