<p><p>The non-invasive glucose monitoring market encompasses two primary types: wearable blood glucose monitoring systems and tabletop blood glucose monitoring systems. Wearable devices, such as smartwatches and patches, offer continuous glucose tracking and provide real-time data without needing blood samples. In contrast, tabletop systems are stationary devices that can analyze glucose levels from interstitial fluid or through advanced imaging techniques, often used in clinical settings for their accuracy and reliability. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management and patient convenience.</p></p>
