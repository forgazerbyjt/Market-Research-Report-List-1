<p><p>The Marine AIS (Automatic Identification System) Monitoring System market can be categorized into two main types: onshore-based and vessel-based. Onshore-based systems are installed at coastal facilities and are used to monitor vessel traffic, enhancing maritime safety and efficiency. They provide real-time data for maritime authorities. In contrast, vessel-based systems are equipped on individual vessels, enabling them to transmit and receive information about their location, course, and speed, thereby facilitating communication among ships and improving navigation safety.</p></p>
