<p><p>The electrical safety switches market consists of various types designed to enhance electrical safety in different applications. Switchboard safety switches are installed within main electrical panels to protect circuits from overloads and faults. Power point safety switches are integrated into wall sockets, preventing electrical shocks by disconnecting power under fault conditions. Portable safety switches are designed for temporary setups, allowing flexibility and additional safety for electrical tools and equipment in various environments. Each type plays a crucial role in safeguarding users from electrical hazards.</p></p>
