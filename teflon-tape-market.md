<p><p>The Teflon tape market is categorized into four main types based on density: low density, standard density, medium density, and high density. Low-density tapes are flexible and suitable for applications with low pressure. Standard density tapes provide a balanced performance for general use. Medium-density tapes offer enhanced durability and are ideal for moderate pressure situations. High-density tapes are designed for high-pressure environments, providing superior sealing capabilities and resistance to various chemicals, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
